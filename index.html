The Google Developer Group Community (GDGC) at our college 
is a student-led organization dedicated to fosteringa collaborative
environment where students and tech enthusiasts can explore,learn,
and build skills in programming,app development, and emerging technologies.
As part of the global GDG network,we focus on creating a supportive space for 
learning Google technologies and other industry-standard tools.
        

Our club organizes workshops, tech talks, and hands-on coding sessions where members 
can deepen their understanding of various topics, including Android development, web 
technologies, cloud computing, artificialintelligence, and machine learning. 
Beyond technical skills, GDGC also encourages networking, collaboration,
and problem-solving, preparing students for success in the tech industry.
        
Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, GDGC welcomes you to join, 
learn, and share your knowledge while building real-world projects and participating
in community-driven events.
